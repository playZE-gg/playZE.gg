Being a zombie is mechanically different from playing as human. You don't shoot: you run, coordinate, and time your attacks.

## Your goal

Infect every human before they reach the escape trigger. You don't need to infect all of them yourself, each human you infect becomes another zombie helping you.

## Speed and mobility

Zombies are fast. Use this. You can catch isolated humans easily. Don't waste time fighting into a well-organized hold, look for flanks and shortcuts when possible.

:::note
You can meta-game a bit on this point by listening to what the human leader is calling out. Sometimes they will say to watchout for a shortcut you might not have known about.
:::

## Targeting

Prioritize:
1. Isolated or slow humans at the back of the group
2. Humans holding an exposed position
3. Item holders (if you can reach them). Removing an item user can flip a hold

## Coordinating with other zombies

Like humans, zombies benefit from coordination:
- Multiple zombies rushing a defense from one direction creates chaos
- Splitting the approach forces humans to cover two angles
- One zombie acting as a distraction while another flanks is a classic pattern

## Zombie-specific abilities

Many ZE maps add zombie-specific abilities/items via [EntWatch](/skills/items-and-entwatch/): a leap, a scream that blinds humans, HP regeneration, or a speed boost. These vary by map and server.

## Winning as zombie

Most ZE rounds favor humans on well-played maps. As a zombie, your win condition is often exploiting human mistakes: late movers, poor positioning, or an item that wasn't used properly. Make sure not to go AFK right after getting infected, try and help your new team!
