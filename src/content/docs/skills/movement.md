ZE movement is source movement, but in a mode where falling behind means getting infected, mastering it matters more than in almost any other context.

## Bhopping (Bunny Hopping)

Bhop lets you maintain or gain speed by jumping repeatedly at the right moment. In ZE it's used to escape from zombies or reach holds faster.

- Jump the moment you land (not before)
- Strafe left/right while in the air (A/D keys + mouse in the same direction)
- Most servers require you to manually bhop, while others may have auto-bhop enabled

:::note[Server-specific bhop rules]
Most servers have a speed cap set, so you can't go as fast as you want by bhopping. Even so, it is usually still faster than just walking.
:::

[Check out this video for a super simple intro to bhopping!](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/ZibCqqVVX9I)

## Surfing

Some ZE maps have surf ramps — angled surfaces you glide across by holding into the ramp at the right angle. Surfing in ZE is typically brief and map-specific, not the extended surf-map style.

- Hold into the ramp (A or D depending on orientation)
- Don't jump off — let the ramp carry you

[Check out this video for a super simple intro to surfing!](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/f3VSdLI45m4)

## Boosting

Players can stand on each other's heads to reach elevated positions. Used on specific maps to access holds faster or to get a player to an item.

- Crouch to give another player a stable surface to stand on
- Communicate before boosting, unexpected boosts cause deaths

:::caution[Use Cases]
This technique is only really used on older version of Counter-Strike, or on 'zm_' maps, which are for the adjacent game mode, Zombie Survival
:::

## Fall damage

CS2 fall damage applies in ZE, sometimes. Most maps actually have fall damage disabled, but this can be re-enabled by server admins. When enabled, large drops that would normally be risky in deathmatch can be lethal in ZE because you may arrive at the hold location damaged.

## Crouch-jumping

Combining crouch + jump lets you clear objects slightly too tall to jump over normally.

## Common movement mistakes in ZE

- Stopping to shoot and falling behind the group
- Attempting to bhop when you don't know how. You will only go slower than walking
- Some maps don't require you to actually complete movement-based sections. Listen to the leader if they say you dont have to surf or do some jumps
